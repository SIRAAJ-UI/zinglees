ABOUT US
THE PROBLEM WITH CURRENT ASSESSMENTS

The current assessment process favors rote learning and focuses mostly on memory evaluation through a series of periodic tests. We believe that the current educational system is missing out on the true purpose of assessments. These assessments are done to provide a deep insight into the learning that the child has achieved. They are only meaningful if the school can evaluate the needs, strengths, and weaknesses of each child to give them the personalized attention that they truly deserve! The traditional assessment process has become redundant and is not relevant according to the needs of children as well. Through our efforts, we aim at revamping the assessment process to give each child the chance to thrive and become successful future leaders of tomorrow.

MAINTAINING EXCELLENT QUALITY THROUGH PERSONALIZED INSIGHTS

With this rapidly evolving educational environment, assessing only based on memory retention is not a good approach. Analyzing their ability to learn and to apply learned methodologies and techniques is extremely important to maintain a global standard quality of education. To reach maximum efficiency and perfection for the next generation of future leaders, we must take a thorough look inwards and diagnose where we are at this point. ZINGLEES was founded to meet the evaluation and assessment needs of the Indian School niche. We work hand in hand with both schools and parents to revamp the evaluation process.
