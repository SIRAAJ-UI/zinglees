ABOUT US
WHY US

With this rapidly evolving educational environment, assessing only based on memory retention is not a good approach. Analyzing their ability to learn and to apply learned methodologies and techniques is extremely important to maintain a global standard quality of education. To reach maximum efficiency and perfection for the next generation of future leaders, we must take a thorough look inwards and diagnose where we are at this point. ZINGLEES was founded to meet the evaluation and assessment needs of the Indian School niche. We work hand in hand with both schools and parents to revamp the evaluation process.

A large portion of schools finds it difficult to achieve their set goals and statistics show that this is primarily due to a mere formal assessment practice that we have in place today. Other than inadequate assessments, there could also be a variety of reasons for schools to fail their targets and goals. With our help, schools can identify, pinpoint, and resolve each hurdle to ensure that they reach all set targets with ease.

TAILOR FIT ASSESSMENT SOLUTIONS

While teaching can be inclusive, Learning is exclusive. Learning varies from student to student based on so many factors that influence the learning of every individual. ZingLEES (Zing Learner Evaluation & Empowerment Solutions), is an emerging exclusive evaluation enterprise for the K-12 segment.

Understanding the understanding of every student is the first key step to ensure learning for every child in every class. We at ZingLEES by associating with schools as evaluation partners, through our purposeful and mindful evaluation process will help schools and teachers have detailed insights of the student’s strengths, needs, weakness, struggle patterns, understanding levels in every subject in every concept.

Understanding the limitations of the schools and the teachers and break away from the monotonous assessment formality which focuses on testing just the rote memory and to support the schools, ZingLEES’s services will squarely meet the expectations set by NEP 2020 in assessing the learning of every child. This will also help the schools in scoring better grades in the SEQI assessment for schools.

Learning learning is the first step to ensure learning.

YOUR TRUSTED EVALUATION PARTNER

Partner with us and allow us to take over your complete evaluation process. We cover daily tests, practice tests, weekly and monthly tests, term tests, revision assessments, and final year exams. We also provide detailed analytics on each child for schools and teachers to accurately monitor their advancement and level of proficiency over a defined period.

IDENTIFYING KEY STRENGTHS AND WEAKNESSES OF EACH CHILD

The primary goal of each parent is to ensure that their children are equipped with the necessary skills to be able to conquer any future challenges and hurdles with ease. However, when it comes to monitoring their child’s education, they leave it solely up to the schools to ensure that their children are successful. Parents depend on test results and notifications from the schools as key identifiers about their child’s performance. The current reports about the performance do not give proper insight into the strengths, needs, weaknesses, and problem areas of the child. Thus parents are not able to intervene when their child needs them the most.
